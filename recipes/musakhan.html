Musakhan..

Musakhan is known as Palestine’s National Dish. It comprises a soft yet crisped-edge flatbread topped with jammy caramelized sumac onions, moist roasted chicken, and toasted nuts. It is the ultimate cozy comfort meal.

Ingredients
  • Chicken
  • Onions
  • Sumac
  • Olive Oil
  • Bread
  • Pine nuts/almonds
